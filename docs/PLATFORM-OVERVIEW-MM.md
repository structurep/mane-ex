# ManeExchange — Complete Platform Overview

## What It Is

ManeExchange is a luxury equestrian marketplace for buying and selling hunters, jumpers, and dressage horses across the US and Canada. It replaces the broken status quo — Facebook groups, word-of-mouth, "DM for price," and handshake transactions — with a structured, transparent, trust-first platform designed for serious equestrians making $30K–$450K+ purchases.

**Target audience:** Women ages 14–80, wealthy, emotionally connected to horses. Trainers, amateur owners, junior riders' families, dealers, and professional programs. Think The RealReal meets Zillow — for horses.

**Competitors:** BIGEQ, HunterJumper Exchange, Mane Street Market.

## 1. User Roles

### 1.1 Buyers
- Browse listings, save horses, compare side-by-side
- Post ISOs (In Search Of) describing exactly what they're looking for
- Receive AI-matched recommendations
- Communicate with sellers through built-in messaging
- Schedule trials, request live viewings
- Make offers through ManeVault escrow
- Review sellers after purchase

### 1.2 Sellers (Individual Owners)
- Create detailed listings with photos, videos, pricing, health records, show history
- Receive inquiries from qualified buyers
- Respond to ISO posts with matching horses
- Track listing analytics (views, saves, inquiries)
- Accept offers and manage transactions through ManeVault

### 1.3 Trainers / Dealers
- Manage multiple listings from one dashboard
- Act as intermediary for clients (sell horses on behalf of owners)
- Browse ISO repository to match buyer requests with horses in their program
- Manage buyer relationships (track which buyers are looking, their preferences)
- Build a public barn profile with program information, staff, and stable photos
- Track standings and analytics across all listings
- Collaborate with other sellers

### 1.4 Service Providers
- **Shippers:** List transport services on the shipper marketplace, receive referral bookings
- **Insurance providers:** List coverage options, integrated into the transaction flow

## 2. Core Features

### 2.1 Mane Score (0–100)
The platform's trust and transparency metric. Every listing gets a score based on:
- Listing completeness (photos, video, description)
- Verified documents uploaded (PPE, x-rays, coggins, vaccination records)
- Show record linked (USEF/USHJA/FEI auto-integration)
- Pricing transparency (actual price listed vs. "contact for price")
- Seller verification status
- Health disclosures

**Purpose:** Buyers can filter/sort by Mane Score. Higher scores = more visibility, more trust, faster sales. This is the platform's **reputation moat** — scores can't be ported to competitors.

### 2.2 ManeVault Escrow
Built-in secure payment system for every transaction. Milestone-based fund release:

| Milestone | % Released | Trigger |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| PPE Approval | 25% | Buyer approves pre-purchase exam |
| Trial Completion | 25% | Buyer completes trial period |
| Delivery Confirmation | 50% | Horse delivered and accepted |

- **Fee:** 5% standard, 4% for Elite sellers
- Protects both parties — funds held until conditions are met
- Full cost transparency: horse price + commission + shipping + insurance shown upfront
- Dispute resolution process built in

### 2.3 ISO (In Search Of) Matching
Inverts the traditional marketplace dynamic:
- **Buyers** post exactly what they're looking for (discipline, budget range, height, age, location radius, specific requirements)
- **Sellers/Trainers** browse active ISOs and submit matching horses directly
- AI matching scores each submission against the ISO criteria
- Trainers can manage ISOs across multiple clients from a single dashboard

### 2.4 AI-Powered Matching & Recommendations
- **Daily Matches:** Buyers receive daily horse recommendations based on their profile, preferences, saved searches, and browsing behavior
- **Smart Recommendations:** "Based on your searches" section on Browse page
- **Match %:** Each listing shows how closely a horse matches the buyer's stated preferences (visible after signup)
- **ISO Auto-Matching:** AI scores seller submissions against buyer ISO criteria

### 2.5 Price Intelligence
- **ManeEstimate:** AI-driven price estimates based on comparable sales, breed, age, training, show record (Phase 1 feature)
- **Deal Ratings:** Great Price / Good Price / Fair Price / Above Market indicators on each listing
- **Price History:** "Price updated X time(s) · Y days on market" — softened view, no exact dollar drops
- **Market Intelligence:** Analytics dashboard showing pricing trends, time-on-market averages, and demand signals

### 2.6 Verified Documents
Sellers upload directly to their listing:
- PPE reports
- X-rays
- Coggins
- Vaccination records
- Registration papers
- Training logs
- Show records (USEF/USHJA/FEI auto-link)
- Vet records

All visible to buyers **before** they inquire. No more "I'll send it later."

### 2.7 Communication
- Built-in messaging system (no more texting, DMs, or email threads)
- Message history tracked — no ghosting without a trail
- Tier-gated: Free users get 5 messages/month, paid tiers get unlimited
- Shared across buyer/seller/trainer conversations

### 2.8 Compare Horses
Side-by-side comparison tool:
- Price, height, age, breed, discipline
- Mane Score
- PPE status
- Days on market
- Save/share comparisons

### 2.9 Saved Searches & Alerts
- Save search criteria (discipline, price range, location, height, etc.)
- Receive notifications when new listings match
- Share shortlists with trainers

## 4. Browse & Discovery

### Browse Page (`/buy`)
- 3-column Zillow-style card grid
- **Filters:** Price (dual-thumb range slider $0–$1M), Age (0–30), Height (12–19hh), Sex (pill toggle), Discipline (pill toggle)
- **Sort:** Featured, Price High/Low, Newest, Most Saved
- **Card hierarchy:** Photo → Price (bold, DM Sans) → Specs line (age | height | sex | discipline) → Horse name + location → FOMO stats (saves/viewers/inquiries as icon+number)
- **Badges:** One per card — "New" or "Popular" or "Featured Seller"
- **Elite seller differentiation:** "Listed by [Seller Name]" on card
- **"Based on your searches"** personalized section below main grid
- Count header: "X horses available · Updated just now"

### Advanced Search
- Extended filters: breed, color, registry, price type, location radius
- Keyword search across descriptions

## 7. Upgrade UX Philosophy

**Never say "Upgrade to Pro."** Show the gap instead:

- **Blurred data:** Analytics preview visible but unreadable without upgrade
- **Grayed compose box:** Message limit hit → compose area grays out with subtle context
- **Lock icons:** Feature visible but locked with small lock indicator
- **Silhouettes:** "X people viewed your listing" without names unless upgraded
- **Timestamps:** "Your listing was viewed 47 times this week" for free → "Sarah J. from Wellington viewed at 2:14 PM" for Pro
- **Trial countdown:** Buyer trial shows remaining days subtly
- **Message limit bar:** Progress bar showing 3/5 messages used this month

Let curiosity sell. LinkedIn/Zillow model — you can see something exists, you just can't access it yet.

## 12. Technical Architecture

### Frontend
- **Framework:** React 18 + TypeScript
- **Router:** React Router v6 (`createBrowserRouter`)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4 (utility-first, custom theme)
- **State:** Zustand stores (userStore, portalStore, offerStore, messagingStore, compareStore, isoStore, savedStore, notificationStore, reviewStore, leaseStore, trainerProfileStore, trainerHorseStore, trainingStore, savedSearchStore)
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Build:** Vite
- **Hosting:** Vercel

### Design System
- **Colors:** Black #050505, Off-black #0A0A0A, White #F2F2F2, Coral #E35F4F (accent only)
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (headlines), Source Sans 3 (body), DM Sans (prices/UI)
- **Border radius:** `rounded-xl` (cards), `rounded-full` (CTAs/pills)
- **No emojis:** All icons from Lucide library
- **No gradients:** Solid colors only

### Backend (Planned)
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Auth:** Supabase Auth
- **Payments:** Stripe (escrow via Stripe Connect)
- **File Storage:** Supabase Storage
- **API:** Supabase Edge Functions + REST
- **ID Verification:** Third-party service (TBD)
- **Registry Integration:** USEF/USHJA/FEI APIs (TBD)

### Current State
- Frontend is **fully built** with mock data (30 horses, all UI pages functional)
- Backend is **not yet connected** (Supabase direct access blocked; schema designed but not deployed)
- Stripe, ID verification, and registry APIs need external setup

## 14. Business Model Summary

**How ManeExchange makes money:**

1. **Sellers pay monthly** to list and sell horses ($0–$149/mo)
2. **Buyers pay monthly** for premium search and matching tools ($0–$39/mo)
3. **ManeVault takes a cut** of every completed sale (4–5%)
4. **Shippers pay to be listed** on the transport marketplace ($99–499/mo + 10% referral)
5. **Insurance providers pay to be listed** ($199–999/mo + 8–15% of premium)
6. **Industry pays for data** — market intelligence and pricing data ($2K–50K/yr)

**Flywheel:** More sellers → more listings → more buyers → more transactions → more data → better AI matching → better Mane Scores → more trust → more sellers.

**Moat:** Mane Score (non-portable reputation), transaction history, review network, AI matching trained on proprietary data.
