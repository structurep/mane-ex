# ManeExchange — Design Philosophy

## Movement: Folded Elegance

### Visual Identity

The ManeExchange brand draws from the Origami design system — precision, restraint, and transformation through careful folding. The logo marries an abstract, geometric horse head rendered in origami-style faceted polygons with refined typography that breathes sophistication.

### Typography

- **Brand Name "Mane"**: Georgia or Noto Serif — weight 300 (light), letter-spacing 0.08em. Elegant serif communicates heritage and trust.
- **"EXCHANGE"**: Helvetica Neue or Inter — weight 300, letter-spacing 0.12em, all caps. Clean sans-serif conveys modernity and platform reliability.
- **Tagline**: Helvetica Neue or Inter — weight 400, 11px, letter-spacing 0.2em, all caps. Whisper-quiet, functional.

### Design Principles

1. **Origami Geometry**: The horse icon uses flat polygon facets with fold creases and layered opacity to evoke paper folding — never photorealistic, always abstracted.
2. **Gold as Trust**: The warm gold palette signals premium quality, heritage, and sophistication — appealing across generations.
3. **Breathing Room**: Generous negative space between icon and type. Nothing crowds.
4. **Restraint**: Minimal elements, no gradients beyond the fold simulation, no decorative extras. The work feels labored over through what it *omits*.

### Usage Notes

- SVG format scales to any size without quality loss — ideal for web
- For print, convert to high-res PNG at your target DPI
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" in Mane
- Gold (#C9A84C) should be the sole accent color across the platform UI
